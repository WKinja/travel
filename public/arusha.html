Arusha

Gateway to Tanzania’s most famous national parks and Mount Kilimanjaro treks.

Description

Arusha is a charming city in northern Tanzania, perfectly located for travelers exploring Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, and Mount Meru. It’s a center for safari tourism with bustling local markets and rich culture.

History

Arusha grew from a small colonial trading post into a modern city and diplomatic hub. It’s known for hosting peace negotiations and being home to the East African Court of Justice.

Attractions

  • Arusha National Park
  • Serengeti Safaris
  • Cultural Heritage Centre
  • Mount Kilimanjaro Treks

Food

Experience Tanzanian cuisine with grilled meats, ugali, and stews. Local favorites include:

  • Blue Heron – Pizza, burgers, and outdoor seating
  • Le Patio – Trendy lounge with local fusion
  • Khan’s BBQ – Popular street grill with flavor-packed plates

Nightlife

  • Via Via Cultural Café – Music, drinks, and local vibes
  • Mambo Club – Late-night spot for dancing
  • Le Patio Lounge – Stylish setting with weekend DJs
