Jinja

Adventure capital of East Africa and source of the Nile River.

Description

Jinja is a vibrant town in eastern Uganda located where the Nile River begins its journey from Lake Victoria. It's a paradise for adrenaline seekers, nature lovers, and those seeking scenic relaxation by the river.

History

Jinja was developed during the British colonial era as an industrial town. Its significance grew after being identified as the Source of the Nile by John Hanning Speke in the 19th century.

Attractions

  • Source of the Nile
  • White Water Rafting on the Nile
  • Bujagali Falls
  • River Cruises & Quad Biking

Food

Fresh tilapia, nyama choma (roast meat), and Ugandan street food are popular. Try these places:

  • The Black Lantern
  • The Deli
  • Bikeventures Café

Nightlife

  • Nile View Casino – Great views and casual gaming
  • Rumours Bar – Classic backpacker’s bar and music
  • Jinja Sailing Club – Drinks, views, and local crowd

Where to Stay

  • Nile River Explorers – Adventure lodge
  • 2 Friends Guesthouse
  • Jinja Airbnbs
