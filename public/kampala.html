Kampala

Uganda’s lively capital with historic sites, hills, nightlife, and rich culture.

Description

Kampala is a bustling city built on seven hills, offering a mix of traditional culture, colonial history, and urban energy. It's the economic and cultural heart of Uganda and offers something for every type of traveler.

History

Initially the seat of the Buganda Kingdom, Kampala became the capital during British colonial rule. It has grown into one of East Africa’s most vibrant and fastest-growing cities.

Attractions

  • Uganda Museum
  • Kasubi Tombs (UNESCO Site)
  • Gaddafi Mosque (National Mosque)
  • Kampala Walking Tours

Food

Don’t miss local dishes like luwombo, rolex (rolled eggs), and grilled meats. Best places include:

  • Cafesserie
  • 2K Restaurant
  • Piato Restaurant – African buffet and grill

Nightlife

  • Sky Lounge – Rooftop drinks and views
  • Guvnor – Kampala’s legendary club
  • Otters Bar – Laid-back lakefront setting
