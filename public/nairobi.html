Nairobi

Kenya’s capital city offering a unique mix of urban life, wildlife, and culture.

Description

Nairobi is a vibrant African metropolis where towering buildings meet lush national parks. Known for its lively markets, diverse food scene, and accessibility to nature, it's a hub for travelers seeking adventure and modern comforts.

History

Nairobi began as a railway depot in 1899 and grew rapidly into a colonial administrative center. Today, it’s a major economic and political capital in East Africa, home to several international organizations and cultural institutions.

Attractions

  • Nairobi National Park
  • Giraffe Centre
  • David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust
  • Nairobi National Museum

Food

Enjoy Kenyan dishes like nyama choma and ugali, or international cuisine in stylish restaurants. Try:

  • Carnivore Restaurant – Iconic for roasted meat
  • Inti – A Nikkei Experience
  • Flamingo Grill – Local favorites with a modern twist

Nightlife

  • K1 Klubhouse – Popular with locals and tourists
  • Brew Bistro – Craft beer and live music
  • 1824 Whiskey Bar – Energetic nightlife with live DJs
