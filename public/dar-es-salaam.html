Dar es Salaam

Tanzania’s largest city, full of culture, coastlines, and commerce.

Description

Dar es Salaam is a vibrant harbor city on the Swahili coast. It serves as Tanzania’s economic and cultural hub with beautiful beaches, lively markets, and a mix of modern and historical attractions.

History

Founded in the 19th century by Sultan Majid of Zanzibar, Dar grew rapidly under German and British colonial rule. Today, it's a dynamic city reflecting influences from Africa, Arabia, and Asia.

Attractions

  • National Museum & House of Culture
  • Kigamboni Bridge
  • Village Museum
  • Coco Beach

Food

Dar offers a fusion of Tanzanian, Indian, and Swahili dishes. Try:

  • Agustino Restaurant
