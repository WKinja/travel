Kigali

Rwanda’s modern, green, and clean capital nestled among rolling hills.

Description

Kigali is a calm and beautifully organized city known for its cleanliness, safety, and scenic landscapes. It offers modern urban experiences with deep cultural heritage and access to Rwanda’s natural wonders.

History

Kigali became Rwanda’s capital in 1962. It’s a city of transformation and healing, especially after the 1994 genocide. Today, it’s a symbol of peace and progress, with memorials preserving national memory.

Attractions

  • Kigali Genocide Memorial
  • Inema Arts Center
  • Kimironko Market
  • Campaign Against Genocide Museum

Food

Try brochettes, sambaza, and isombe. Notable restaurants include:

  • Heaven Restaurant
  • Papyrus – Local fusion in a laid-back setting
  • Meze Fresh – Burrito bar with African twist

Nightlife

  • Rooftop bars like Ubumwe Grande Hotel Lounge
  • Papyrus – Chill music and cocktails
  • Choma’d – Trendy hangout for youth and visitors

Where to Stay

  • Kigali Marriott Hotel
  • Heaven Boutique Hotel
  • Kigali Airbnbs
