Watamu

A coastal gem offering a blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and relaxing charm.

Description

Watamu is a tranquil beach town between Malindi and Kilifi, known for its turquoise waters, powdery beaches, and rich biodiversity. Part of the Watamu Marine National Park, it’s a protected area teeming with sea turtles, coral reefs, and vibrant marine life. The laid-back environment makes it perfect for family vacations, romantic getaways, and solo exploration.

History

Watamu’s history is deeply rooted in Swahili culture, with influences from Arab traders and Portuguese explorers. The nearby Gede Ruins are remnants of a once-thriving 13th-century Swahili town. These archaeological remains speak to Watamu’s importance as a trading hub along the Indian Ocean coast.

Attractions

Visitors are drawn to the Watamu Marine Park for snorkeling, diving, and glass-bottom boat tours. Mida Creek is ideal for kayaking and birdwatching, while Arabuko-Sokoke Forest is a paradise for nature lovers.

  • Watamu Marine Park
  • Mida Creek
  • Arabuko-Sokoke Forest
  • Gede Ruins

Food

Watamu’s dining scene reflects its Swahili heritage with strong Italian influences. Must-try dishes include seafood platters, samaki wa kupaka (coconut fish), and homemade gelato. Top places to eat include:

  • Papa Remo – Beachfront Italian & Swahili fusion
  • Crab Shack Dabaso – Rustic mangrove setting
  • Ocean Sports Restaurant – Casual dining with ocean views

Nightlife

Watamu nightlife is laid-back and vibrant. Enjoy beach bonfires, acoustic music, and DJ sets under the stars. Popular spots include:

  • Come Back Club – Local club with dancing and drinks
  • Lichthaus Watamu – Rooftop bar with sunset views
  • Papa Remo – Occasionally hosts beach parties and music nights

Where to Stay

Whether you’re looking for luxury or budget, Watamu has you covered. Recommended stays include:

  • Hemingways Watamu – 5-star resort with spa and ocean views
  • Medina Palms – Stylish boutique suites
  • Turtle Bay Beach Club – All-inclusive family resort
  • Airbnb Watamu – Private beach houses and villas
